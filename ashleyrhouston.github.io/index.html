Ashley Renée Houston

Boston, MA · a.houston@northeastern.edu | ashley.houston@bc.edu

Medical Anthropologist and Social Epidemiologist.

I'm interested in identifying root causes of health inequities to aid in their prevention. I examine how social and economic inequities influence distributions of health. Topically, I focus on migration and structural and institutional forms of oppression--particularly in policy, labor and employment, health care, and housing. Methodologically, I draw on my interdisciplinary background and training in ethnographic and qualitative methods to epidemiological, quantitative, and mixed methods.

Outside of teaching and research, you can find me reading, gardening, crafting, catching live music, or organizing for a brighter future.
