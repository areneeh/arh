Service

    Creating community is a personal value and something I prioritize where I spend my time and in the places I live. Throughout my doctorate and postdoctoral fellowship, I have been involved in developing and organizing ways for colleagues to come together across departments.

    In 2020, I co-lead a book club with students and faculty at Northeastern interested in reading social justice texts. In 2019, I co-organized a Graduate Student Summit that brought more than ten departments together to co-learn. Throughout 2018-2020, I co-organized a Graduate Student Coffee hour across campus. In 2018, with colleagues, I started the Population Graduate Student Association and the Population Health Seminar to allow doctoral students to practice presentations, offer feedback, and workshop ideas.
